# 🤗 Hugging Face Platform Overview (Beginner Guide)

---

## 📌 What is Hugging Face Platform?

Hugging Face is a platform where you can:

* Use pre-trained AI models 🤖
* Explore datasets 📊
* Build and share AI apps 🧪

👉 It is like **GitHub for AI models**

---

## 🧭 Understanding the Homepage

When you open Hugging Face, you see different sections.

---

## 🔹 "All" Section

* Shows everything in one place:

  * Models
  * Datasets
  * Spaces
  * Community content

👉 Think of it as: **"Show me everything"**

---

## 🔹 Top Menu (Important 🚀)

### 🧠 Models

* Collection of AI models
* Example:

  * Chatbots
  * Text generators
  * Image models

👉 Most important section for beginners

---

### 📊 Datasets

* Data used to train models
* Example:

  * Text data
  * Images

👉 Used when training your own model

---

### 🧪 Spaces

* Live AI applications
* Built using:

  * Gradio
  * Streamlit

👉 You can:

* Try projects
* Build your own apps

---

### 📦 Buckets (New Feature)

* Used for storing files
  👉 Not required for beginners

---

### 📚 Docs

* Official documentation
  👉 Use when you need help

---

## 🔹 Other Sections

### 👥 Following

* Shows content from people you follow

---

### 🔥 Trending

* Popular models and apps

👉 Best place to explore new things

---

### ➕ New Button

* Create:

  * Model
  * Dataset
  * Space

---

## 🎯 Quick Summary

* Models = AI tools
* Datasets = Data
* Spaces = Live apps
* All = Everything together
